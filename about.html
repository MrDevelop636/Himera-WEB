Our Journey

Key milestones in HIMERA's evolution

1987

HIMERA Founded

Alexandra Chen establishes HIMERA with a small team in a garage, focused on high-performance engineering.

1995

First Concept Car

Unveiled the H1 Concept at Tokyo Motor Show, showcasing innovative aerodynamics and hybrid technology.

2008

Electric Revolution

Committed to full electrification with the launch of our first all-electric sports car, the E-Series.

2020

Sustainability Pledge

Announced carbon-neutral manufacturing and 100% renewable energy commitment across all facilities.

2023

Autonomous Future

Launched Neural AI driving system, setting new standards for safety and autonomous capabilities.
